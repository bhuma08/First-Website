    Cupcake

    List of Ingredients

    • 125g caster sugar
    • 125g butter, softened (or margarine)
    • 125g self-raising flour
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1tsp vanilla essence

    Cream the butter, sugar and vanilla extract togehter in a bowl

    Add the eggs and flour

    Beat everything together until the mixture is smooth

    Use a tablespoon to half fill each cakecake cases witht he cupcake mixture

    The cupcakes should only take 20 minutes to cook

    Take the cakes out of the oven when they are golden shade

    Enjoy!

    Peanut Butter Cups

    List of Ingredients

    • 3 tbs powdered sugar
    • 120g peanut butter
    • 170g melted chocolate

    Stir peanut butter and powdered sugar together until smooth

    Spread 1 to 2 tbs of chocolate in the bottom of each cupcake liner

    Put 1 to 2 teaspoons of peanut butter mixture on top of the chocolate

    Cover the peanut butter with more chocolate and smooth out the top

    Refrigerate for 1 hour until chocolate has hardened

    That's it, Peanut Butter Lovers!
